This dataset corresponds to a dynamic response-recovery curve
of a polyaniline (PANI) film carried out with controlled ammonia 
exposure between 50 and 500 ppm. 

Each exposure cycle was set to 15 min and and the recovery to 45 min.
By setting the visualization parameters starting from 3 and setting
start to zero, the exposure routine information is described in 
the table below:


conc (ppm)	startExpTime	EndExpTime	EndRecTime
50.00		15		30		75
100.00		75		90		135
200.00		135		150		195
300.00		195		210		255
400.00		255		270		315
500.00		315		330		344
