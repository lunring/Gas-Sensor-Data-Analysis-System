This dataset corresponds to four different rGO-based sensors
whose electrical resistance was recorded by controlled
exposures of NO2, between 0.5 and 5 ppm. Each exposure cycle
lasted 10 min and the recovery 50 min.

By setting the visualization parameters between 310 and 650,
the exposure routine information is described in the table below:


cycle	conc	startTime	EndRespTime	EndRecTime
1	0.5	50		60		110
2	1	110		120		170
3	2	170		180		230
4	5	230		240		290
